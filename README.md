# Zomato Data Analysis (EDA Project)

## Project Overview
This project presents an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the Zomato restaurant dataset to understand the factors influencing restaurant ratings, customer preferences, pricing trends, and service-related patterns.

The analysis focuses on how variables such as cost, location, cuisine, online ordering, and table booking impact restaurant performance and customer satisfaction.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains restaurant-related information, including:

- Restaurant ratings  
- Cost for two people  
- Cuisines  
- Location / area  
- Online ordering availability  
- Table booking availability  
- Reviews and other restaurant attributes  

---

## ⚙️ Steps Performed

### Data Cleaning
- Handling missing values  
- Formatting columns  

### Feature Engineering
- Creating cost categories  
- Extracting primary cuisine  

### Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Data Visualization  
- Using Matplotlib & Seaborn  

---

## Key Insights
- Most restaurants have ratings between **3.5 and 4.2**  
- A large number fall in the **low to medium cost range**  
- Higher cost shows only a **slight improvement in ratings**  
- A few cuisines dominate customer preferences  
- Online ordering and table booking are associated with better ratings  
- Restaurants with more reviews have more stable and reliable ratings  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on mid-range pricing to attract a wider customer base  
- Offer online ordering to improve convenience and reach  
- Prioritize popular cuisines to reduce business risk  
- Choose locations carefully *(high demand = high competition)*  
- Improve food quality, service, and ambience  
- Encourage customer reviews to build trust and visibility  

---

## Conclusion
The success of a restaurant depends on a balanced combination of affordability, convenience, service quality, and customer satisfaction.

No single factor guarantees success, but the right mix of pricing, location, cuisine, and service can significantly improve performance.
